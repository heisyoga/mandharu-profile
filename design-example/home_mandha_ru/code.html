EST. 1984

Our Story: Brewing Nostalgia

Mandha Ru was born from the desire to recreate the feeling of "Pulang" — coming home. In a world that moves too fast, we invite you to sit, linger, and reconnect with the simple joys of a perfectly brewed cup.

Our beans are sourced from heritage plantations that have sustained families for generations. We honor these traditions through slow-roasting techniques that preserve the soul of the coffee.

"Every corner of Mandha Ru is a tribute to the warmth of a grandmother's kitchen and the quiet strength of old wood."
